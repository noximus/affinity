the kings of nyc at the bike film festival

Staffan Jofjell had stumbled over a photograph in a café in East Berlin text fpo dedicated to the journalist Egon Erwin Kisch. It pictured hardy cyclists lining up for the 1925 fpo tk edition of the 6-Tage-Rennen together with vibrant texts of Kisch, “the raging reporter”. Tkfpo He immediately became fascinated of what seemed to be the ultimate test of man and machine. Text here fpo Bicycles stripped down to their essence, men and women ready to prove their endurance in the setting of a city that had always fascinated him.
